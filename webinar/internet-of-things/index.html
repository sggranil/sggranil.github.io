Internet of Things: Endless Possibilities

I. Details of the event/activity

The webinar "Internet of Things: Endless Possibilities" conducted by Team Pixelplay on December 22, 2022 is a great opportunity to learn about the latest developments in the field of Internet of Things (IoT). The resource speaker, Mr. Juan Paulo Pineda, IT Manager at Pricon Microelectronics, provides an overview of the current IoT landscape and discuss how to leverage the technology to gain competitive advantages. He also shared practical implementations of IoT applications and discuss the potential of this technology to transform businesses and our lives.

II. Reflections on the webinar/activity give rise to (learnings)

This webinar was a great experience for me as it gave me a comprehensive understanding of the possibilities of the Internet of Things, and I am very glad that this webinar is quite the most successful one because other students from different department from Gordon College, and other participants from other school showed interest in joining with Sir Paulo as he teaches how IoT evolves and used in other fields, especially in medical, and IT field also.
Mr. Juan Paulo Pineda's presentation was very informative, and he provided insightful examples of how IoT can be used in different industries. His knowledge of the subject was extensive, and he was able to answer all of the questions asked by the audience. It was also interesting to hear about the applications of IoT that are being developed in the present and future.

III. Reflections on possibilities on implementing some of the webinar input on a practical level (practical application of learnings)

I found the webinar input on IoT to be very informative and helpful. It gave me insight into the possibilities of integrating IoT into my own projects with the use of Arduino as a beginner. I was particularly interested in the potential of leveraging existing infrastructure and the idea of using a "hub and spoke" approach to create an interconnected system of smart devices.
