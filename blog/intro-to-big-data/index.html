Introduction to Big Data and Analytics

I. Details of the event/activity

The Community Ignite Series was a collection of webinars conducted by Edukasyon.ph, and co-presented by AWS and AWS Siklab Pilipinas. The 67th installment of said webinar was entitled: “Introduction to Big Data and Analytics” which held last November 24, 2022 from 6PM to 7PM. Mr. Kyle Escosia, a data platform engineer from Aboitiz Data Innovation, imparted his knowledge about data analytics.

II. Reflections on the webinar/activity give rise to (learnings)

I learned that big data and analytics have become increasingly important in recent years, as more and more organizations are recognizing the value of using data to inform their decision making. As far as I know, one of the key trends in this area is the growth of cloud-based data storage and processing solutions, which make it easier and more cost-effective for organizations to store and analyze large amounts of data. Another trend is the increasing availability of advanced analytics tools and techniques, such as machine learning and artificial intelligence, which are making it possible for organizations to gain insights from their data in new and more powerful ways. Additionally, there is a growing focus on data privacy and security, as organizations look to protect their data and the sensitive information it contains. Overall, it is an exciting time for the field of big data and analytics, and it will be interesting to see how these trends continue to evolve in the coming years.
I also realized that big data has also become an increasingly important aspect of many different industries, including banking, healthcare, education, and government. In the banking sector, big data is often used to analyze customer behavior and identify patterns that can be used to improve financial products and services. In healthcare, big data is being used to develop more effective treatments and to improve the overall efficiency of the healthcare system.
In education, big data is being used to track student performance and identify areas where additional support may be needed. This can help educators to tailor their teaching methods and improve student outcomes. In the government, big data is being used to analyze trends and patterns in a variety of areas, such as crime, healthcare, and transportation. This can help government agencies to make more informed decisions and to better serve the needs of the public.
Overall, the application of big data in these different sectors is helping to improve the way that services are delivered and to drive more efficient and effective operations. As the amount of data continues to grow, the potential for big data to transform these industries will only continue to expand.

III. Reflections on possibilities on implementing some of the webinar input on a practical level (practical application of learnings)

It is clear for me that the use of big data and analytics has the potential to greatly improve decision-making and operational efficiency in a wide range of industries. By analyzing large and complex datasets, organizations can gain valuable insights and better understand the factors that drive their success. This can help them make more informed decisions, improve their products and services, and ultimately become more competitive in the marketplace.
One possible way to implement some of the ideas discussed in the webinar is to invest in the necessary technology and infrastructure to collect, store, and analyze large amounts of data. This may involve implementing a data warehouse or other data management system, as well as investing in tools and software for data mining and analysis.

IV. Positive feedback (on the event organization)

I found the material to be well-organized and engaging, and the speaker was knowledgeable and provided valuable insights. I particularly appreciated the real-world examples and case studies that were used to illustrate the concepts. Overall, I feel like I have a much better understanding of the basics of big data and analytics, and I am excited to continue learning more about this fascinating field.
